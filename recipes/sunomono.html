Sunomono (Japanese Cucumber Salad)

Description

Japanese sunomono is delicious and simple to make with thinly sliced cucumbers in a sweet and sour vinegar dressing for a refreshing salad or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers, peeled
  • ⅓ cup rice vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise and scoop out any large seeds. Slice crosswise into very thin slices.
  3. Place vinegar, sugar, ginger, and salt in a small bowl; mix well to combine. Add cucumber slices and stir to coat.
